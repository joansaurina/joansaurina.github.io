Joan Saurina i Ricós


I graduated in Artificial Intelligence from the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), including international exchange semesters at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) and Stanford University.

During my B.Sc., I completed research internships at Stanford University (2023-2025, Alexander G Ioannidis), the Barcelona Supercomputing Center (BSC) (2024-2025, Dr. David Torrents), and the Institute of Industrial and Control Engineering (IOC) (2023-2024, Prof. Isiah Zaplana).

Currently, I am a research intern at the European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI) under the supervision of Jess Ewald, where I am integrating large language models with Cell Painting data to predict drug toxicity.
